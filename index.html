This Privacy Policy explains how Purview LLC ("we", "us", "our"), the developer of the Thyme mobile application (the "App"), collects, uses, and protects information in connection with your use of the App.

Thyme is built to be privacy-preserving by design. We do not require an account, we do not ask for your name or email to use the App, we do not run advertising, and we never sell or share your personal information. Most of what you create stays on your device or in your own private iCloud, which we cannot read.

3. Screen Time & Focus Shields

Thyme uses Apple's FamilyControls, DeviceActivity, and ManagedSettings frameworks so you can voluntarily block distracting apps during focus periods.

  • All Screen Time data stays on your device in an App Group container and is never sent to any server, including ours.
  • We cannot see which apps you select. Apple's tokens are opaque — the App cannot read app names, bundle IDs, or any identifying detail from them.
  • No usage monitoring. We do not record your screen time, app activity, screenshots, or recordings.
  • Individual (personal) authorization only. We do not use parent/guardian or Family Sharing modes.

4. On-Device Intelligence

Thyme's natural-language task entry and smart scheduling run entirely on your device using Apple's on-device Natural Language framework. Your task text is never sent to us or to any third-party AI service for processing. These features do not access your Screen Time data.

Our scheduling suggestions are automated but assistive only — they arrange tasks in your day and do not produce legal or similarly significant effects. You remain in control and can change any suggestion.

5. Service Providers (Processors) & Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information for advertising. We rely on a small number of providers strictly to run the App:

  • Apple Inc. — iCloud/CloudKit stores your synced content in your own private database; the App Store and StoreKit process any purchases. Your use of iCloud and the App Store is also governed by Apple's privacy policy.
  • [ANALYTICS PROCESSOR — e.g. "TelemetryDeck GmbH (Germany)"] — receives the anonymous install identifier and usage events described above to produce aggregate, privacy-preserving analytics. Chosen because it does not build advertising profiles.

7. Data Retention

  • Your content is kept until you delete it in the App, delete the App, or remove it from iCloud. Deleting a task removes it locally and from your iCloud on next sync.
  • The anonymous install identifier persists in the keychain until you delete the App and clear its keychain data.
  • Analytics events are retained by our analytics processor for no longer than needed for the purposes above, per its retention limits.
  • On-device logs are capped and overwritten automatically.

8. Your Rights Under the GDPR / UK GDPR

If you are in the EEA, the UK, or Switzerland, you have the right to: access your personal data; rectify inaccurate data; erase your data ("right to be forgotten"); restrict or object to processing; data portability; and to withdraw consent at any time (for example by revoking Screen Time permission in iOS Settings) without affecting prior processing.

Because Thyme has no account system, you can exercise most rights directly: your content lives on your device and in your private iCloud, so you can view, edit, export (via iOS/iCloud), and delete it yourself at any time. For anything you cannot do in-app, contact us at artstalkerhere@gmail.com and we will respond within the time limits required by law (generally one month).

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

9. Your Rights Under the CCPA / CPRA (California)

This section applies to California residents under the California Consumer Privacy Act, as amended by the CPRA.

Categories of personal information we collect

  • Identifiers — the anonymous install identifier (a random UUID; not your name, email, or account).
  • Internet or other electronic network activity — usage events generated as you use the App.

We collect these from your interaction with the App, for the business purpose of operating, maintaining, securing, and improving it. We do not collect the other statutory categories (real name, contact info, financial account numbers, precise geolocation, biometric information, or browsing history outside the App).

Sale and sharing

We do not and will not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising, as those terms are defined under the CCPA/CPRA. We have not done so in the preceding 12 months. Because we do not sell or share, there is nothing to opt out of — but you may still contact us to confirm.

Sensitive personal information

We do not collect or use sensitive personal information for the purpose of inferring characteristics about you, so the "right to limit" does not apply.

Your California rights

  • Right to know / access the categories and specific pieces of personal information we hold.
  • Right to delete personal information we have collected.
  • Right to correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights.

To exercise these rights, email artstalkerhere@gmail.com. Because we hold no account and only an anonymous identifier, we may be unable to associate a request with a specific individual; where we can, we will verify your request by reasonable means before acting. You may use an authorized agent to submit a request on your behalf.

10. Children's Privacy

Thyme is not directed to, and is not intended for, children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Our use of FamilyControls is limited to the individual (personal) authorization mode and does not involve child accounts or Family Sharing. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

11. Security

Your content is protected by your device's security and by Apple's iCloud and keychain protections. Payments are handled entirely by Apple; we never receive or store your payment details. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we design the App to keep as little data as possible off your device.
