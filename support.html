Frequently Asked Questions

My focus shield isn't blocking apps

Check three things, in order:

  1. Settings → the shield's app category is still granted under Screen Time access (Thyme will show a banner if this was revoked).
  2. The habit or focus session is actually inside its scheduled window — shields only activate during the time you configured.
  3. If you recently changed the apps/categories being blocked, force-quit and reopen Thyme once so the change propagates to the system shield extension.

If shields still don't fire, email us with the habit's schedule and roughly when it should have activated.

How do I manage, upgrade, downgrade, or cancel my Thyme+ subscription?

All billing is handled by Apple, not by us — we never see your payment details, so subscription changes happen in your Apple ID settings, not in the App:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top, then Subscriptions
  3. Select Thyme+ and choose to change plan or cancel

You can also jump there directly from Thyme: Settings tab → Thyme+ → Manage Subscription. Cancelling stops future renewals but keeps your current billing period active — see our Terms of Service for the auto-renewal details.

I paid but Thyme+ isn't unlocked

Open Settings tab → Thyme+ → Restore Purchases. This re-checks your Apple ID's purchase history and re-applies any active entitlement. If it still doesn't unlock after that, email us — include the approximate purchase date and which plan (monthly, annual, or lifetime).

How do I delete my data?

Thyme doesn't have accounts or sign-in, so there's no "account" to delete — but here's how to remove your data completely:

  • Individual tasks or habits: delete them directly in the App at any time.
  • Everything on this device: delete the Thyme app (press and hold the icon → Remove App). This clears all local data immediately.
  • Everything in iCloud: Settings app → your name → iCloud → Manage Account Storage (or Manage Storage) → find Thyme → delete its data. This removes your synced content from Apple's servers.

If you'd like help doing this, or want confirmation that a specific data type is gone, email us and we'll walk you through it. See our Privacy Policy for exactly what we collect and where it lives.

Does Thyme see which apps I've chosen to block?

No. Apple's Screen Time frameworks give us only opaque, unreadable tokens for your selections — we cannot see app names, categories, or any of your device activity. Details in the Privacy Policy, §3.
